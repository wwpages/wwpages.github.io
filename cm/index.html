Clarksville Monstrosity @ West Windsor

Docs:
  • The Stipulation of Settlement and Consent Order between West Windsor Township, NJ and Atlantic Realty, concerning the development of warehouses at Clarksville and Quakerbridge Roads in West Windsor. 6.8 MB, 136 pages. Source: West Windsor Township.
  • Legible site plan (hi-res). Source: West Windsor Township.
  • Resolution 2020-R247 authorizing the settlement, unanimously adopted by Council in an empty room on Nov. 30, 2020. VIDEO of 8.5 minute discussion and unanimous vote.
  • Ordinance 2020-25 authorizing the creation of the commercial district for the warehouses, unanimously adopted by Council on a near-empty Zoom call on Dec. 14, 2020. VIDEO of 3.5 minute discussion and unanimous vote.
