Comcast viewers may watch both live and pre-recorded West Windsor Cable Television programs on Channel 27, as well as School and Municipal Bulletin Boards.

Verizon viewers may watch pre-recorded West Windsor Cable Television programs along with School and Municipal Bulletin Boards on Channel 42.  Live broadcasts from West Windsor are only available to Verizon viewers on Channel 41.

Visit the West Windsor Township website to view recorded Town Council Meetings and other municipal events.
