Chilling Conclusion: The eastward Big Gulp

Finally, the Planning Board, again acting unilaterally, on its own without the recommendation of Burgis Associates, decided to apply the "effective redevelopment" rule to seize all four remaining lots to the east of Route 1. The reason given was that "more comprehensive redevelopment" was necessary. This was the final, big gulp of land eaten by The Planning Board Blob.

Of course, this inclusion, too, was not at all necessary to redevelop the area, rather serving only to maximize the profits of the speculator(s) holding the land, and the developer(s) acquiring it.

The Planning Board Blob having eaten everything there was to eat, it ceased its land-devouring activities.
